How Do We Protect Your Information?

PrimeVortex uses secure technologies and industry-standard security measures to protect customer information.

Security measures include:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption

  • Restricted access to sensitive information
  • Continuous security monitoring and maintenance

All information submitted through our website is handled with appropriate security measures to help protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Do We Disclose Any Information To Outside Parties?

PrimeVortex does not sell, trade, rent, or transfer personally identifiable information to outside parties.

Exceptions may include trusted parties such as:

  • Trusted business partners

Such parties are required to maintain confidentiality and use the information solely for authorized purposes.
